<h2> Why drugs? </h2>
<p>
Why do people take drugs? Most people that are addicted to drugs use it only because it gives then a feeling of momentary<mark> happiness or ecstasy</mark>. But in the long run drugs play a very bad role in your body.
</p>
</section>

<section>
<h2> Ways to prevent the usage of drugs</h2>
<ul>
<li>avoiding places where you know drugs and alcohol will be available.</li>
<li>surrounding yourself with friends who don't use drugs.</li>
<li>knowing how to resist temptation.</li>
<li>learning how to cope with stress and relax without drugs.</li>
<li>distracting yourself with activities like exercise or listening to music.</li>
</ul>
